Positions

What we believe in


Our lives can only prosper if we maintain a life and market free of any government barriers. These barriers must be taken down in order for our nation to remain the best developed and affluent superpower. Only when this happens can our nation truly experience what the founding fathers had envisioned; a free and prosperous state.

Social Security

Believe in an "opt out" policy in which one can choose to privatley invest (we belive this to be the better option) or go with a government sponsored social security plan.

Jobs

Free market should dictate the job market.

Economy

100% Free Market

Security/Defense

Believe in reducing nuclear arms in the U.S. The military should be use to protect people's livery and property only.

Tax Reform

Support tax cuts, low intrest rates in an effort to stimulate the economy.

Immigration

Generally support closed or tight borders and tracking system for foreign travelers. Along with an easier legal process for those who wish to get in.

Faith

Strict adherence between the seperation of church and state. Pormote secular issues and a more secular nation.

Education

Promote school choice/vouchers and homeschooling.

Abortion

Generally pro-choice, with limits set.

Energy

Wish to find enviornmentally friendly energy sources and solutions.

Health Care

Support where there is both a private and public option, and the people have a choice on which they desire to use.

Foreign Policy

End most foreign aid, and only keep aid that is absolutely necessary.

Campaign Finance Reform

Favor more regulation with spending limits on individuals and corporations.

Enviornment

Strong support of the envoirnment.

Guns

Limited gun control.

Gay rights

Pro private choice and equality including marriage.

About the Party

The Greenback Party is an economic reform party that wants to get rid of government intervention, and let the free market decide the ecomonmy. We believe in the equality of all people, the rights to all citizens, and a better world for tomorrow.
