Who we are?

We are the Greenback Party. An economic protest party focused on economic prosperity through no government intervention in our free market capitalist system. We also believe in a government that should step down its social spending and tax rates to allow for a 100% free market to run at peak efficiency. Healthcare and social security must gradually become privatized in order to acquire a fully free market. The government should not provide these expenditures but instead become a competitor of the market as well. Our military and national defense can benefit with private competition in order to protect life and property. Although we support a free market, we also will try to aim towards an environmentally “clean” market. This will be achieved through supporting private and public research towards environmental problems and advising businesses to opt for practical, environmental solutions. There will be no government regulations, as we believe the market will correct itself but if a practical “clean” solution is viable, we advise it. We are a party that is towards maintaining freedom but also actively socially responsible for the good of the nation.

About the Party

The Greenback Party is an economic reform party that wants to get rid of government intervention, and let the free market decide the ecomonmy. We believe in the equality of all people, the rights to all citizens, and a better world for tomorrow.
