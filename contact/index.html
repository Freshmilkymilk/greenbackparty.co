About the Party

The Greenback Party is an economic reform party that wants to get rid of government intervention, and let the free market decide the ecomonmy. We believe in the equality of all people, the rights to all citizens, and a better world for tomorrow.

About the Website

This website was made by Michael Anderson for American Government at Falcon Highschool. The party itself does not represent a real party, but the ideals of this "made-up" party are those of a group a students taking the American Government class.
